--How to run the program--
Executable opens a GUI for the user.

Mergesort_Time.csv will be placed in program
directory at that time. 

Left side of GUI contains a selection box to 
select desired array for display. Unsorted and 
sorted arrays are displayed in in labeled 
fields to the right.

Select desired array and click 'Select Array'.

Click 'X' in top right corner to exit.

--How was the spreadsheet generated?--
Mergesort_Time.csv is generated automatically
by the program at runtime.
